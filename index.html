About Us

At Shiatsu Care, we've spent over 15 years helping people relieve pain, reduce stress, and regain mobility through focused deep-tissue bodywork. Unlike general massage studios, we specialize in authentic Shiatsu acupressure, reflexology, and cupping therapy to target the root causes of discomfort—not just the symptoms.

Body Acupressure

We go beyond Swedish-style surface massage. Our intensive Shiatsu approach reaches deep muscle layers to release chronic tension and improve posture.

Foot Reflexology

Our foot massage goes beyond simple rubbing. By stimulating key reflex zones, it helps restore balance, reduce fatigue, and revitalize your entire system.

Cupping

More than just relaxation — our cupping therapy uses targeted suction to stimulate circulation, detoxify the body, and release deep-seated tension.

Our services

Body Acupressure

Traditional oriental massage technique that uses thumbs, fingers, and hands to apply pressure.

  • Boosts energy flow

No oils. No needles. Just healing touches.

foot reflexology

A complementary therapy that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet,known as reflex zones, which correspond to different systems in the body.

  • Reduces stress and promotes relaxation
  • Relieves soreness and tension
  • Improves circulation
  • Enhances Sleep Quality

cupping therapy

A method where special cups are placed on your skin and pulled to create a suction. This suction gently pulls the softtissue layers to improve blood flow and promotes relaxation.

  • Increase circulation
  • Relax muscles
  • Relieves muscle tension and soreness
  • Reduce stress and anxiety
